For our team’s second choice, we have another known brand among the others in this list. The testers liked the consistent texture throughout the whole product, and the fact that it’s not lumpy at all pleased this part of our team.

The next thing that was pleasing for our experts was that the official website of this lip plumper provided users with a complete ingredients list. Our specialists were slightly disappointed when there was no list of active ingredients offered by the manufacturers, yet they found one ingredient quite interesting; capsicum frutescens fruit extract.

Capsicum frutescens fruit extract, also known as capsicum annuum fruit extract, contains a chemical substance called capsaicin, a chemical substance found in hot peppers. This substance increases the blood flow in the area it has been applied on, and there have been reports of additional firmness due to using this substance. Although this chemical substance can increase circulation giving your lips a plumping effect, it might cause irritation as well for people with sensitive skin.

In the end, our testers did notice plumper and firmer lips, and they also really liked that the product came in various color options though they would have appreciated it if the brand didn’t use chemical pigment.

Moving on, we have the Charlotte Tilbury collagen lip bath in third place on the list. This lip plumping gloss made our volunteer testers a bit excited due to the glossy, glittery look of it. They were delighted with the plumper’s consistency, and the glitter gave their lips more volume visually.

This plumper also succeeded in pleasing our team of experts by using soluble collagen or just collagen, an ingredient quite known for its incredible skincare effects. Soluble collagen is one of the natural proteins found in the skin and is the cause of the skin’s firmness. As we age, the levels of collagen decrease, and it starts to break down. This fantastic substance smoothens and softens the skin giving your skin a healthy, youthful look while binding water to your skin which highly hydrates your skin in return. Also, soluble collagen can act as a filler and soften your wrinkles and fine lines or adding volume, as well as making the area it’s been applied to become firmer.

The product is reasonably priced, which ultimately pleased both our experts and volunteer testers, and our testers also liked the different color options, even though they didn’t like the fact that the pigments were chemical.
